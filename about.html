GYM & Fitness

Fitness enthusiasts can enjoy access to modern gyms equipped with state-of-the-art workout machines, professional trainers, and wellness programs. Whether you're into weight training, cardio workouts, or yoga sessions, the mall's fitness centers provide a space to maintain a healthy lifestyle.

Virtual Reality Games

Step into the future of entertainment with Virtual Reality (VR) gaming experiences at Mombasa Mall. The VR gaming zones offer interactive and immersive games, ranging from adventure, racing, and multiplayer battles to virtual reality escape rooms. It's a fun-filled experience for both kids and adults looking for high-tech excitement.

Healthcare

Mombasa Mall provides top-tier healthcare services, including pharmacies, medical clinics, dental care, and wellness centers. Visitors can access professional consultations, emergency medical assistance, and routine health check-ups, ensuring that their health needs are met conveniently within the mall.

Salon and Haircuts

For those looking to refresh their look, the mall features high-end salons and barber shops offering a variety of hair treatments, styling, and grooming services. Whether it's a quick trim, a stylish makeover, or a relaxing spa session, professional stylists ensure you leave looking your best.
